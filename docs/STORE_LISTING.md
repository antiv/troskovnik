## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Troškovnik turns the paper receipts you already get into a private, searchable
expense log — without sending your data anywhere.

Scan the QR code on any Serbian fiscal receipt and Troškovnik fetches the full
details straight from the Tax Administration portal (suf.purs.gov.rs): merchant,
date, items, taxes and total. Everything is saved locally in an encrypted
database on your phone. No account, no cloud, no tracking.

WHY TROŠKOVNIK

• Private by design — all data stays on your device, encrypted (AES-256).
  We run no server and collect nothing.
• Fast capture — scan the receipt QR and you're done; manual entry as a backup.
• Never lose a receipt — even if the merchant hasn't posted the items to the
  tax server yet, the receipt is saved and its items are filled in automatically
  later.

KEY FEATURES

• QR receipt scanning with automatic data retrieval from suf.purs.gov.rs
• Encrypted local storage (SQLCipher / AES-256) — your data never leaves the phone
• Receipt list with search by merchant or item, sorting and filters
• Business vs. personal tagging and filtering
• Warranty tracking — set a warranty per item or per whole receipt, with a
  default 24-month consumer term; get reminded 30 and 7 days before it expires
• Keep the receipt as proof of purchase (the journal, the official verification
  link, and an optional photo) — because paper receipts fade
• View and share your proof photos
• Spending analytics — totals, by month, by merchant, business/personal split,
  most frequent items and estimated VAT
• Serbian (Cyrillic and Latin) and English

PRIVACY

Troškovnik does not contain ads, analytics or third-party tracking. The only
internet connection it makes is directly to the official Tax Administration
portal to read the receipt you scanned. See our Privacy Policy:
<PRIVACY_POLICY_URL>

Troškovnik is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the
Serbian Tax Administration (Poreska uprava).
```

## Data safety form (answers)

Use these when filling Google Play's **Data safety** section:

- **Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?** → No.
  (All data is processed and stored only on the device; nothing is sent to us or
  any third party.)
- **Is all user data encrypted in transit?** → Not applicable to us — we
  transmit no user data to our servers. The only outbound request is the user's
  own receipt verification to the Tax Administration over HTTPS.
- **Is data encrypted at rest?** → Yes (on-device, SQLCipher / AES-256).
- **Do you provide a way to request data deletion?** → Data is local; users
  delete it in-app or by uninstalling. No server-side data exists.
